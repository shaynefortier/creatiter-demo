F.A.Q.

What is Creatiter?
Creatiter makes 2D animations for films, short videos, and TV shows for geeky young adults. It broadcasts their work on Internet as streaming service for cheap. The free animation are uploaded on YouTube. It streams legal anime series too by working with Funimation for translation and dubs.
Why working on 2D and not 3D?
3D animation is a great technology to work with. CGI and 3D have a lot of use. It is even understandable 3D is more mainstream than 2D animation for films, because the technology that brings more realism. However, it isn't the technology that makes a good film. It is the great storytelling and the artistic aspect of animation people loves. Traditional and 2D animation art style is unlimited. It is even greater when 3D and 2D are combined together. In short, we believe that 2D animation has still room for improvement and Creatiter wants to push beyond with the today standard. Creatiter wants the 2D to follow the modern society.
Why broadcasting anime while Crunchyroll and Funimation already does?
Creatiter is a french canadian business, so it offer French and English service for all canadian. Anime was never available for french canadian, because France keeps the adapted version for themself. Thus, a french canadian have to go the pirated anime streams to watch them in French or they have to learn English. Crunchyroll has French translations, but the library is very limited besides the ones in France. Creatiter parterned with Funimation to get the licenses neccesary to dub anime into French canadian version so that they can watch their favorite anime legally and supporting the anime industry.
